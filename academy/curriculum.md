# 📚 Sanae Academy Curriculum

This curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between AI technology and inclusive architectural design for the next generation.

## Module 1: Empathy in Design
* **Goal:** Understanding neurodiversity and sensory triggers.
* **Activity:** Sensory mapping of a local school or hospital.

## Module 2: AI as a Tool for Inclusion
* **Goal:** How to use the SanaeSpace engine to scan 2D blueprints.
* **Activity:** Using our [thresholds.json](../data/thresholds.json) to identify "Red Zones" in a layout.

## Module 3: Gamified Social Responsibility
* **Goal:** Designing inclusive neighborhoods using gamified AI simulations.
* **Activity:** Team challenge to redesign a public park for autistic children.
